About

Welcome to the Gnana Jyothi Residential School website. We are a part of the GVS Trust(R), which has had a strong presence on the education map of Karnataka. The Trust has been associated with education since 1984. Today we are a fully fledged CBSE and Karnataka State affiliated school with student strength of more than 1000 and staff more than 100. We are committed to excellence in education from KG to PUC. This website has been created with the sole purpose of giving complete information about the school. We believe that every parent has a right to know details about our school, which we try and provide here. The website is organized in such a way that new parents seeking admission into the school, can either get an overview of the admission process, or get detailed information about the school.



Gnana Jyothi Residential School,is an CBSE and State co-educational Residential and day-school set up under GVS Trust(R) in 1983-84. We follow the State syllabus and CBSE combined with Activity based and Student friendly teaching methods to meet the student needs.
